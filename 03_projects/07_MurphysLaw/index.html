Keymap of video Control

  • [Shift]+Space - "play/pause"
  • [Shift]+P - decrease playback speed.
  • [Shift]+U - increase playback speed.
  • [Shift]+R - reset playback speed to 1.0x.
  • [Shift]+P - rewind video by 10 seconds.
  • [Shift]+N - advance video by 30 seconds.
